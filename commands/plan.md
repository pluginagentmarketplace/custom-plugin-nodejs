# /plan

Create a personalized learning path tailored to your goals, current level, and available time.

## Usage

```
/plan
/plan backend-developer
/plan react-specialist
/plan 6-months
/plan fullstack intensive
```

## Description

Get a structured, personalized learning roadmap that transforms your goals into actionable steps with timelines, milestones, and resources.

## Planning Features

### Goal-Based Path Creation
Specify your goal and we create a customized plan:
- **Role-Based** (e.g., "Backend Developer", "DevOps Engineer")
- **Skill-Based** (e.g., "Master React", "Learn Go")
- **Timeline-Based** (e.g., "3 months", "6 months", "1 year")
- **Intensity-Based** (e.g., "Part-time", "Full-time", "Intensive")

### Personalization Factors
- Your current skill level
- Available study hours per week
- Learning style preference
- Technology preferences
- Career goals and timeline
- Budget constraints

## Customization Options

### Duration Options
- **Accelerated**: 1-3 months (intensive)
- **Standard**: 3-6 months (balanced)
- **Relaxed**: 6-12 months (part-time)
- **Custom**: Your preferred timeline

### Intensity Levels
- **Part-time**: 10-15 hours/week
- **Full-time**: 30-40 hours/week
- **Intensive**: 40-60 hours/week
- **Custom**: Your availability

### Learning Style Preferences
- **Visual**: Videos, diagrams, visualizations
- **Hands-on**: Coding projects, exercises
- **Theory-first**: Concepts before implementation
- **Blended**: Mix of all approaches

### Resource Preferences
- **Free**: Open-source, free courses
- **Paid**: Premium courses, books
- **Blended**: Mix of free and paid
- **Academic**: University-level resources
