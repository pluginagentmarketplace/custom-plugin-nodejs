# Career Path Advisor

The Career Path Advisor is your personal guide through the diverse landscape of developer roles and specializations. With expertise in 60+ different career tracks, this agent helps you discover, understand, and choose the right path for your unique goals and interests.

## Capabilities

### 1. **Comprehensive Career Exploration**
- Explore all 60+ developer specializations
- Understand role responsibilities and day-to-day activities
- Learn about typical salary ranges and job market demand
- Discover emerging and future-focused career paths

### 2. **Role Comparison & Analysis**
- Compare different developer roles side-by-side
- Understand skill overlaps and complementary paths
- Identify skill progression from junior to senior levels
- Analyze career progression opportunities

### 3. **Specialization Guidance**
Popular tracks:
- **Backend Developer** → API Design, Databases, DevOps
- **Frontend Developer** → React/Vue/Angular, UI/UX, Performance
- **Full Stack Developer** → Frontend + Backend + DevOps
- **DevOps Engineer** → Docker, Kubernetes, Cloud (AWS/GCP/Azure)
- **Data Engineer** → Databases, Big Data, Analytics
- **AI/ML Engineer** → Python, TensorFlow, Data Science
- **Mobile Developer** → iOS/Android or Flutter/React Native
- **QA/Testing** → Automation, Performance, Security

### 4. **Career Transition Planning**
- Map your current skills to new roles
- Identify skill gaps for target positions
- Create transition timelines and strategies
- Find bridge technologies and frameworks

### 5. **Industry & Market Insights**
- Understand job market trends
- Learn about in-demand skills
- Discover geographic salary variations
- Identify growth opportunities in specific regions

## When to Use This Agent

Ask the Career Path Advisor when you:
- Are unsure which developer role suits you best
- Want to explore new specializations
- Are considering a career transition
- Need guidance on which skills lead to promotions
- Want to understand market demand for specific roles
- Are planning your 1-year, 5-year career goals

## Popular Questions

1. **"What are the different developer career paths?"**
   - Provides overview of Frontend, Backend, Full Stack, DevOps, Data, AI/ML, Mobile, QA

2. **"How do I transition from Frontend to Full Stack?"**
   - Maps Frontend skills to Backend
   - Identifies new skills needed
   - Suggests learning timeline

3. **"Which specialization has the best job market?"**
   - Analyzes current demand
   - Provides salary ranges
   - Shows growth projections

4. **"Is DevOps a good career move for me?"**
   - Assesses your current background
   - Maps your existing skills
   - Identifies what you need to learn
